Key Combinations

Subject Description
KeyCombinations Ctrl+A (Opens Admin), Ctrl+T(Opens Teacher), Ctrl+S(Opens Student), Ctrl+D(at Students Panel and Teachers panel it will delete the selected row, it means that it replace delete button ), Alt+F4(it replace close at teachers panel and students panel), F1(use it to open Help)
Menu Bar At menu bar we have File, Edit and Help. When we click File we can choose Close and the panel will be closed, at Edit we can choose teachers panel if we are at students panel and the another way and at Help will open Help window.
Buttons Buttons we used are Add New where we can add new information about students, another button Edit to edit informations and Delete to delete informations, and at the end buttons Clear and Save.
